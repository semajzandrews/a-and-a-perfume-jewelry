## The ONE concept — "Composed by hand"
A perfume-and-jewelry gift shop is, at heart, a place that *composes a gift in
layers*: a scent has a top, a heart, and a base; an adornment catches light in
facets. The site treats the shop as an artisan's apothecary-cabinet where a gift
is built up layer by layer. Warm parchment ground, cacao-brown ink, brass-thread
hairlines, Boska's high-contrast serif doing the "label" voice.

## Signature moment (industry-rare, screenshottable)
**The Olfactory Layer Pyramid** — a scroll-built, interactive scent-note pyramid.
As the band enters view, three horizontal strata (TOP / HEART / BASE notes) draw
in and fill, each a stratum of a signature gift composition. Hovering or tapping a
stratum lifts it and reveals its notes (bergamot+amber top, rose+jasmine heart,
oud+vanilla base) with the paired adornment idea. It pairs the two halves of the
shop — scent and jewelry — into one "gift, composed in layers" object. Degrades to
a static labeled pyramid under prefers-reduced-motion and with JS off.
